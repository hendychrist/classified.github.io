How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Input your information, and the app will store it on Firebase Cloud Storage.

Log-In

Enter your registered email and password.

Product Registration

Register your product by providing details such as title, quantity, product image, description, and price.

Product Showcase

Your product will appear on the home or feed page, allowing users to make purchases directly from the app.

Loaded With Features You Would Absolutely Love.

Here, we don't use APIs to store the data Therefore, I utilize Google's product, Firebase Cloud, to store data and assist in the development of this application.

Cloud Based

Cloud Firestore is a flexible, scalable database for mobile, web, and server development from Firebase and Google Cloud.

Payment Gateway

I used The Stripe Flutter SDK that allows to build delightful payment experiences in your native Android and iOS apps using Flutter. And provide powerful and customizable UI screens and elements that can be used out-of-the-box to collect your users' payment details.

Search Menu

Users can utilize the search menu functionality to seamlessly explore an extensive array of products featured within the app, facilitating a convenient and efficient process to locate and acquire the specific items they desire.
